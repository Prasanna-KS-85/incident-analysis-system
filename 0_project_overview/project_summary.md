# Final Project Workflow

## Multi-Agent Architecture
The system employs a team of 5 specialized agents working collaboratively to process citizen grievances:

1. **Classification Agent**
   - Categorizes the incoming grievance into predefined domains (e.g., civic, legal, administrative).

2. **Sentiment Agent**
   - Analyzes the emotional tone and intensity of the complaint text.

3. **Urgency Agent**
   - Assesses the time-sensitivity and potential impact of the grievance.

4. **Decision Agent**
   - Synthesizes inputs from other agents to calculate the Complaint Criticality Index (CCI) and make a final prioritization decision.

5. **Explanation Agent**
   - Generates human-readable explanations for the prioritization decisions made by the system.
